Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Barsys Technologies, Inc. ("Barsys," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website happyhour.barsys.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit the Site or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you submit an inquiry through our booking wizard or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, and office address.

Event Details: We collect event-related preferences such as guest count, package selection, cocktail preferences, and scheduling information to prepare your quote.

Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with the Site, including pages visited, time spent, scroll depth, clicks, browser type, device type, IP address, and referring URL.

Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies from the following services:

  • Google Tag Manager & Google Analytics (GA4) — Site analytics and conversion tracking
  • Google Ads — Conversion tracking and remarketing
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram) — Conversion tracking and audience building
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag — B2B conversion tracking and remarketing

UTM & Ad Parameters: We capture URL parameters (such as utm_source, utm_medium, gclid, fbclid) to attribute inquiries to specific marketing campaigns.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your event inquiries and prepare custom quotes
  • To coordinate and deliver your corporate happy hour event
  • To analyze Site usage and improve our services
  • To measure advertising effectiveness and optimize campaigns
  • To build remarketing audiences for relevant advertising
  • To communicate service updates or promotional offers (with your consent)

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Google, Meta, LinkedIn, and other analytics/advertising platforms that process data on our behalf
  • Business Operations: Payment processors, scheduling tools, and communication platforms necessary to deliver our services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process

4. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, typically no longer than 3 years after your last interaction with us. Analytics data is retained according to each platform's default retention settings.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of targeted advertising and remarketing
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

California Residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

6. Opt-Out Options

  • Google: Google Ad Settings
  • Meta: Facebook Ad Preferences
  • General: Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • Browser: You can disable cookies in your browser settings

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 21 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
